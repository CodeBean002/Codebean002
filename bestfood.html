How to prepare fried Plantain and Egg

Let get started

    STEP ONE

    Peel the plantain and wash before slicing it.
  1. Slice your Plantain into your desired size
    but it shouldn't be too big
  2. Light up your cooking gas
  3. Place your frying pan on fire
    make sure the frying pan is dry before placing it.
  4. Add Oil to the frying pan once you feel that its hot.
  5. If the oil is hot then
    start frying your plantain batch by batch if its much
    don't fry all at once to avoid error
  6. While frying make sure you turn both sides of each plantain to avoid getting burnt.
  7. When the plantain is brownish yellow and soft you can now take out the fried plantain
  8. That is how to fry plantain
  9. Plantain Is ready to be served....
